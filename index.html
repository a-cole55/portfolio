Services

If you're interested in having a custom website made, here's some details on next steps:

  • Consultation: Contact me via email here. We will then schedule a meeting to discuss your vision for the site. This is a great time to share any website examples that you have and we can discuss your likes and dislikes.
  • Proposal: Based on our consultation, I will draft a proposal outlining the project costs, timeline, and details.
  • Develop: We will begin the very exciting process of building your site! During this stage, I will provide frequent updates on the progress of your site.
  • Launch: Once the site is complete and you are satisfied, we will go live and launch your site using the platform of your choice.
  • Support: For an additional fee, I can periodically update your site and take care of ongoing maintenance.
